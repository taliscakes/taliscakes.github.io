Pastry is my passion.

After majoring in Biology and later working for several years as a computer programmer in the high-tech industry - I've decided to now focus on what I really love - baking and creating. I am mostly self taught and can spend hours browsing through pastry books and learning new techniques.

I'm a graduate of Tante Marie's pastry course in San Francisco and have participated as a recipe tester for the book - Chocolate Obsession by Michael Recchiuti.

Many of my birthday cakes and cupcakes are decorated with hand made chocolates .

All my cakes, cookies etc. are made from scratch, and with the finest ingredients (but of course!). And yes, they taste as good as they look.

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area with my loving husband and 3 children, who are always happy to be my chief recipe testers!!!

Aside from making cakes I also enjoy photographing them and maintaining this website.
